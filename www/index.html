Regional Rail Station Arrivals & Departures

This App returns a list/queue of trains scheduled to arrive at a station in two/both directions. The direction is demarcated as either Northbound or Southbound.



Please be aware that usually, no trains run after 12:00, as a result, the app will not retrieve info after that particular time.
